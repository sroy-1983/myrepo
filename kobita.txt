amade die ja ek muthho roddur
ami seke nebo dhusar haat.
amar sukie jaoa chamra
ar ei chhonnochhara jibon.
sritir bhare roktakto ami
sare na kichhutei purano khoto.
amake purie jai bishader sur.

amake die ja ekmutho bosonto
himel haoa santo hobe mon
ure jak joto paap joto glani
chokher dristi gholate, porchhe chhani
bhalobasar adhar peyechhi onek
kintu valobaste parini.
jibon sayanne ese ami khuje jai
sudhu sobujer gondho.

aake die ja ekfonta sisir
bhorer kuasar sathe makhie nebo gaye
modhur smritir vela vasie debo ek fonta jole
valobasar fagun rong hoe gachhe fike
ja chhilo tej aaj sob e ostachole
sob hariechhi aaj choker joler maayay.
bartho ghame sikto amar sara sorir.
